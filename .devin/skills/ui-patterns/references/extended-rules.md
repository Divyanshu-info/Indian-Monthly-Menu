# UI-Patterns: Extended Rules

> Additional rules for pricing, testimonials, and footer.

## Pricing Tables

- **DO**: Offer 3 options: Basic, Pro (highlighted), Enterprise.
- **DO**: Visually emphasize the recommended option: elevated card, accent color, "Most Popular" badge.
- **DO**: Toggle for monthly/annual with visible savings.
- **DO**: Feature comparison as an expandable table below the pricing cards.
- **DON'T**: More than 4 pricing tiers -- decision paralysis sets in.
- **DON'T**: Hidden costs. Transparency builds trust.

## Testimonials and Social Proof

- **DO**: Real quotes with name, role, company, and photo.
- **DO**: Use proven formats:
  - **Logo Bar**: Customer logos in a horizontal row. For B2B trust.
  - **Quote Cards**: Individual testimonials in cards. Emotional impact.
  - **Scroll Marquee**: Horizontally scrolling testimonials. Currently popular on Godly.
- **DO**: Numbers where possible: "10,000+ users", "4.8/5 stars", "97% satisfaction".
- **DON'T**: More than 6 testimonials visible simultaneously.
- **DON'T**: Generic quotes lacking specifics. Concrete results are more convincing.

## Footer

- **DO**: Structured footers with clear columns: Product, Company, Resources, Legal.
- **DO**: Secondary CTA or newsletter signup in the footer.
- **DO**: Social media links as an icon row, not as text links.
- **DON'T**: Footer without legal notice/privacy policy links (legally required in DE/EU).
- **DON'T**: Footer longer than 2 viewports. Accordion on mobile.
